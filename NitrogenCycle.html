More Info & Scientific Resources

The following resources and peer-reviewed studies by Dr. Timothy Hovanec and colleagues form the scientific basis for modern nitrifying bacteria understanding:

  • Primary Resource: Dr. Tim's Aquatics - Fishless Cycling Guide
  • Hovanec, T. A. and E. F. DeLong. 1996. Comparative Analysis of Nitrifying Bacteria Associated with Freshwater and Marine Aquaria. Applied and Environmental Microbiology Vol. 62, No. 8: 2888-2896.
  • Hovanec, T. A., L. T. Taylor, A. Blakis and E. F. DeLong. 1998. Nitrospira-Like Bacteria Associated with Nitrite Oxidation in Freshwater Aquaria. Applied and Environmental Microbiology Vol. 64, No. 1: 258-264.
  • Burrell, P. C., C. M. Phalen, and Timothy A. Hovanec. 2001. Identification of Bacteria Responsible for Ammonia Oxidation in Freshwater Aquaria. Applied and Environmental Microbiology, Dec. 2001, p. 5791-5800.
