Scientific Insight

Scientific products like NH₄Cl are used because they are far more stable than "cleaning ammonia," which degrades over time.

The "2.56" Rule

Most hobby test kits (API, Salifert) measure Total Ammonia (TA) using the Total Ion Scale.

If you dose 2 ppm on the Nitrogen Scale (standard for Dr. Tim's/Fritz), your test kit will read 2.56 ppm.

Ammonium vs Ammonia

Total Ammonia = NH₃ (Toxic) + NH₄ (Ammonium). In standard aquarium water, the majority of your reading is actually Ammonium (NH₄), which is the form provided by Ammonium Chloride.
